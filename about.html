About Us

Our Story

Shiksha Abhiyan Educational Society was founded in February 2009, registered under the Societies Act (Regn. No. S/64796), by Mr. Sandeep Gudiyal (President) and a dedicated group of volunteers in Chanakya Place, Janak Puri, New Delhi. Our mission: “No child left behind—education and empowerment for every underprivileged individual.” We began by offering free tuition to 30 students in 2009. Over the years, we have:

  • Expanded free tuition to over 1,200 students annually.
  • Planted more than 6,000 saplings in South-West Delhi.
  • Organized 25+ free medical camps with local doctors and nurses.
  • Trained 600+ women in tailoring, computer literacy, and financial planning.

Vision & Mission

Vision: A corruption-free, educated, and empowered society where every child and woman has equal opportunities.
Mission: To uplift underprivileged communities in Delhi through education, health initiatives, environmental stewardship, and women’s empowerment.
