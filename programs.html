Free Tuition & Scholarship Programs

We believe every child deserves a quality education. Under this program:

  • Free Coaching: Volunteers (graduates) teach core subjects (Math, Science, English, Hindi) to students of grades 6–12.
  • Stationery Kits: Twice a year, we distribute notebooks, pens, textbooks, and backpacks—absolutely free.
  • Career Guidance: Quarterly workshops on career paths (engineering, medicine, civil services) and scholarship opportunities.
  • Success Stories: Last year, 30 students from our program secured admission to reputable colleges in Delhi.

Plantation & Awareness Programs

Our environmental initiatives aim to create a greener Delhi and educate citizens about sustainable living. Key features:

  • Tree Plantation Drives: Since 2018, we have planted over 6,000 saplings in slum clusters, parks, and schools across South-West Delhi.
  • Community Workshops: We conduct monthly awareness camps on solid waste segregation, rainwater harvesting, and plastic reduction in local markets.
  • Gaushala Seva: Collaborative efforts with local gaushalas to assist in caring for stray cows, feeding them and raising awareness.
  • Environmental Education: We partner with schools for special assemblies on “Save the Environment” and encourage student-led green clubs.

Free Medical Camps & Health Awareness

Good health is the foundation of a productive life. We run:

  • Medical Check-Up Camps: Quarterly camps in collaboration with local clinics and doctors. Services include general physician consultation, basic blood tests, and free medicines.
  • Health Workshops: Sessions on diabetes, hypertension, maternal‐child health, and first-aid training.
  • COVID-19 Support: Mask distributions, vaccination awareness, and home isolation guidelines during peaks (as needed).
  • Pediatric Camps: Free immunization drives for children under 5 in partnership with Municipal Health Department.

Women Empowerment & Anti-Corruption Workshops

We believe in uplifting women through skill training, financial independence, and civic rights awareness:

  • Skill Training Workshops: Tailoring, handicrafts, computer basics, and financial literacy classes for women aged 18–45.
  • Self-Help Groups (SHGs): We facilitate the formation of SHGs and connect them with microcredit programs under NUMLM (National Urban Livelihood Mission).
  • Anti-Corruption Seminars: Sessions on RTI, consumer rights, and how to report public grievances. We also provide a legal aid helpline for women facing workplace harassment.
  • Health & Hygiene Awareness: Special camps on maternal health, sanitation, and nutrition for women in low-income neighborhoods.
