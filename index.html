About Shiksha Abhiyan

Established in 2009, Shiksha Abhiyan Educational Society is a registered NGO (Reg. No. S/64796) in New Delhi. Our mission is to empower marginalized communities through free education, health initiatives, environmental stewardship, and women empowerment. We believe in transparency, accountability, and sustainable impact.

Free Tuition & Stationery
